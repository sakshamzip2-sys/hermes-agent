# Connectors

Knowledge Work Agent draws on the tools each business role depends on. These are the
connectors the source knowledge-work plugins reference per function; wire the ones your
team actually uses (the individual kw- skills list their own placeholders).

- Productivity / project tracking: Slack, Notion, Asana, Linear, Jira, Monday, ClickUp, Microsoft 365
- Sales / CRM: HubSpot, Close, Clay, ZoomInfo, Fireflies
- Customer support: Intercom, HubSpot, Guru
- Product / design: Figma, Amplitude, Pendo
- Marketing: Canva, Ahrefs, SimilarWeb, Klaviyo
- Data / analytics: Snowflake, Databricks, BigQuery, Hex, Definite

Connector calls that spend money, send, publish, or write to a system of record are gated:
the agent drafts and routes, a qualified human approves. Deep finance and legal workflows are
handled by the Finance and Legal profiles, not here.
